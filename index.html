Day 1

Les Houches -> Les Contamines

5 hr 30 min walking. Hotel night. 650 m ascent / 1,300 m descent. Meet at 9 AM at the Prarion car park, head toward the Bellevue cable car, cross Col de Tricot and Alpage du Truc pastures.

Day 2

Les Contamines -> Roselend

7 hr walking. Hotel night. 1,450 m ascent / 800 m descent. Roman road, Col du Bonhomme, Col de la Croix du Bonhomme, then Les Chapieux valley at the end of the Tarentaise.

Day 3

Roselend -> Courmayeur

6 hr walking. Hotel night. 650 m ascent / 900 m descent. Transfer, climb toward Mont Blanc, cross Col de la Seigne from France into Italy, then descend to Courmayeur.

Day 4

Courmayeur -> Bonatti -> Courmayeur

6 hr walking. Hotel night. 1,050 m ascent / 540 m descent. Climb toward Bertone refuge, traverse to Bonatti refuge, descend toward Arnouva, taxi transfer back to Courmayeur.

Day 5

Courmayeur -> La Fouly

6 hr 30 min walking. Inn night. 850 m ascent / 950 m descent. Taxi transfer from Courmayeur, climb with views of Mont Dolent, cross Grand Col Ferret into Switzerland, descend to La Fouly.

Day 6

La Fouly -> Champex -> Trient

Inn night. Option 1: 715 m ascent / 925 m descent / 5 hr. Option 2: 915 m ascent / 1,260 m descent / 7 hr. High traverse above Martigny and the Rhone valley; possible Fenetre d'Arpette route depending on conditions.

Day 7

Trient -> Le Tour

5 hr walking. 1,000 m ascent / 850 m descent. Return to France, descend with Chamonix valley views, finish in Le Tour, then train back toward the Prarion car park around 4:30 PM.

July 13

Chamonix -> Zermatt, then Riffelsee if weather allows

The Zermatt strategy is to use the first good weather window immediately: town walk first, then Rotenboden, Riffelsee, and Gornergrat in late afternoon light.

  • Amy hotel: Hotel Garni Testa Grigia, Zermatt. Group lodging noted as VRBO on Wiestistrasse / Zer Baennu.
  • Classic photo target: Riffelsee Matterhorn reflection.
  • Ride the Gornergrat Railway toward Rotenboden and Gornergrat.
  • Weather logic: Alps often flip between morning sun / afternoon cloud or morning cloud / afternoon clearing, so use the first good Riffelsee window.
  • Late-train note: regular 2026 summer schedule shows a last train down from Gornergrat around 19:18, Rotenboden around 19:25, reaching Zermatt around 19:59; extra sunset trains may run on some dates.
  1. 14:00
    Arrive Zermatt and check in

    Take a quick town walk: Hinterdorf, Bahnhofstrasse, and the church square.

  2. 16:00
    Go up toward Rotenboden

    Walk 5-10 minutes to Riffelsee for the classic Matterhorn reflection angle.

  3. 18:00
    Continue to Gornergrat

    If the sky is still good, keep going higher for the viewing platform and broad Matterhorn panorama.

  4. 19:30
    Train back down

    Return to Zermatt around 8:15 PM if following the suggested late-afternoon route.

July 14

Matterhorn sunrise, Glacier Paradise, and Riffelsee sunset

This is the full Zermatt highlight day: Kirchbrucke alpenglow, Matterhorn Glacier Paradise, Schwarzsee, then a second chance at Gornergrat and Riffelsee.

  • 05:30-06:30: Kirchbrucke for Matterhorn alpenglow.
  • 08:30-15:00: Matterhorn Glacier Paradise, Glacier Palace, observation platform, and Schwarzsee.
  • 16:00-19:30: Riffelsee reflections and golden-hour views if weather is good.
  1. 05:30
    Kirchbrucke sunrise

    Walk from town and watch the first warm light hit the Matterhorn.

  2. 08:30-15:00
    Matterhorn Glacier Paradise

    Go up to the high viewing platform, visit Glacier Palace, photograph 4000-meter peaks, then descend toward Schwarzsee.

  3. 16:00-19:30
    Gornergrat + Riffelsee

    Return to the Gornergrat Railway corridor for the best reflection and evening-light window.

If the weather is excellent

Priority order from the PDF: July 13 afternoon Riffelsee first, then Gornergrat. July 14 morning Kirchbrucke alpenglow first, then Glacier Paradise. This covers three distinct landscapes: Matterhorn sunrise, high glacier world, and Riffelsee reflection.

If choosing one daytime Zermatt attraction

Choose Matterhorn Glacier Paradise before Rothorn or Sunnegga, because the afternoon is already reserved for Gornergrat/Riffelsee and Glacier Paradise gives a different high-glacier view.

Alpenglow timing and viewpoints

The PDF notes typical Zermatt alpenglow as starting around 05:45, with the Matterhorn summit turning golden around 05:50-06:10 and the light moving downward after about 06:15. Best town viewpoint: Kirchbrucke, because it faces the Matterhorn, has the river foreground, and is reachable on foot. Hinterdorf is good for old wooden houses and Swiss village atmosphere; Sunnegga is a higher-view option if the first train timing works, but usually not for true sunrise.

Lucerne stop details

Use station lockers at Lucerne Railway Station. Typical locker cost noted as CHF 6-12 depending on size. Suggested loop: arrive Lucerne station, walk across Kapellbrucke, explore Old Town streets, climb to Musegg Wall viewpoint, and walk back along the river. Total distance is about 2 km. Optional quick Lake Lucerne boat ride is covered by the Saver Day Pass.

Musegg Wall is singled out because it is only 10-15 minutes uphill from the station, gives panoramic views of the old town and lake, takes about 30-45 minutes total, and fits between trains. Look for red rooftops, Kapellbrucke, sailboats on Lake Lucerne, and mountains like Mount Pilatus.

Lucerne highlights

Top sights from the PDF: Kapellbrucke, a 14th-century covered wooden bridge with paintings; Old Town squares with painted buildings, cobblestone streets, and fountains; Lake Lucerne waterfront; and the Lion Monument.

Zurich evening details

Old Town route: Bahnhofstrasse -> Lindenhof Hill -> St. Peter Church -> Limmat River -> Grossmunster -> Fraumunster. Dinner along the Limmat works well for Zurcher Geschnetzeltes, rosti, or Swiss lake fish. Finish with Lake Zurich promenade from Burkliplatz, then Sprungli or Laderach for chocolate.

Zurich hotel from the PDF: Arlette Am Hauptbahnhof Hotel, single room, check-in July 15 at 2:00 PM and check-out July 16 at 11:30 AM. Confirmation code and phone number are omitted from the public site.
