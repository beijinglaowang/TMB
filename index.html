Day By Day

Itinerary

Zermatt Priorities

The Matterhorn plan keeps one clear idea: use weather windows quickly.

1

July 13 afternoon

Arrive, check in, walk Hinterdorf and Bahnhofstrasse, then aim for Rotenboden, Riffelsee, and Gornergrat before evening.

2

July 14 sunrise

Start at Kirchbrucke for Matterhorn alpenglow, then breakfast and reset before the high-altitude day.

3

July 14 daytime

Ride up to Matterhorn Glacier Paradise for glacier views, the observation platform, Glacier Palace, and Schwarzsee.

4

July 14 evening

Return to the Gornergrat Railway corridor if the sky is good and chase Riffelsee reflections in golden light.

Practical Notes

Logistics

TMB Group

7-day guided group: July 5-11, 2026. Meet around 9:00 AM at the parking lot in front of the Prarion cable car, Les Houches.

Includes shared lodging, meals, luggage transfer, mountain guide, first-day cable car, and final local train ticket.

Chamonix and Les Houches

July 4 lodging is Chalet Hotel du Bois in Les Houches. July 11 lodging is ibis Styles Les Houches Chamonix.

Storage note: pick up stored luggage from the July 4 hotel after the trek; estimate 30 minutes round trip on foot.

July 15 Scenic Rail

Zermatt 07:06 -> Visp 08:19 -> Andermatt 10:20. Explore Andermatt, continue to Lucerne, then Zurich HB by 15:55.

Seat notes: left side leaving Zermatt; right side for the Andermatt to Lucerne gorge segment.

Lucerne and Zurich

Use station lockers in Lucerne, walk Kapellbrucke, Old Town, and Musegg Wall, then continue to Zurich.

Zurich evening: Bahnhofstrasse, Lindenhof, churches along the Limmat, lake promenade, and chocolate shopping.
